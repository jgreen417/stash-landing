Stash — Compact Solution Pitch
Stash is a personalised AI shopping and loyalty optimisation platform that helps consumers achieve their shopping goals
while helping sellers design better loyalty programs and reach the right customers.
Today, consumers are surrounded by loyalty programs, vouchers, cashback offers, points, cards, and promotions, but most people do
not know which ones are actually worth using. Existing solutions mostly store cards, show offers, or promote one retailer’s own
program. Stash goes further: it learns each user’s goals, habits, budget, favourite stores, reward preferences, receipts, and spending
behaviour, then tells them the best next action.
For customers, Stash becomes a personal AI rewards agent. It helps them save money, earn better rewards, avoid fake deals,
prevent expired points, detect missed value, and choose the right store, offer, loyalty card, payment card, and redemption moment.
Instead of saying, “Here are your offers,” Stash says:
“Shop here, activate this offer, use this card, ignore that deal, and redeem later.”
The key customer value is simple:
Stash makes every loyalty program work for the customer, not just the company.
For sellers, Stash becomes an AI loyalty intelligence and customer-retention platform. It helps retailers, brands, banks, card
issuers, and loyalty programs reach customers when their offers are genuinely relevant. Sellers get better exposure, better targeting,
stronger conversion, lower wasted promotion spend, customer segment insights, competitor benchmarking, campaign performance
data, and AI recommendations for improving their loyalty programs.
The seller value is:
Promote smarter, not louder.
Stash matches offers with the customers most likely to value them, then gives sellers privacy-safe insights into what rewards actually
drive acquisition, retention, and repeat purchase.
Core product
Stash has three main layers:
1. Consumer AI layer
A personal AI that understands each user’s shopping goals and recommends the smartest way to shop, save, earn, redeem, and avoid
waste.
2. Reward intelligence layer
A system that calculates the real value of points, cashback, vouchers, discounts, cards, expiry dates, basket prices, and overspending
risk.
3. Seller intelligence layer
A platform that helps sellers create better offers, target the right customers, improve loyalty design, and measure retention
performance.
Why Stash wins
Stash is not just an aggregator. Aggregation is only the data layer. The real product is personalised decision intelligence.
Card wallets store cards.
Retailer apps promote one ecosystem.
Cashback apps push affiliate offers.
Bank apps only see payment data.
Generic AI assistants lack loyalty-specific data and reward valuation.
Stash combines:
personal goals + spending behaviour + loyalty programs + offer rules + receipt data + reward valuation + seller insights +
AI recommendations.
Final pitch
Stash is the AI decision layer for the loyalty economy. It helps consumers shop smarter and use every loyalty program
efficiently, while helping sellers design better rewards, reach the right customers, and improve retention. Consumers get
personalised shopping guidance; sellers get smarter promotion and loyalty intelligence. The result is a two-sided platform
where loyalty becomes less fragmented, less wasteful, and more valuable for both sides.
